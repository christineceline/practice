Hi, I'm Christine, and I'm a front-end web developer.

I'm an expat from Germany, now based at the lovely south coast of England.
I love creating new things from scratch and tend to get lost in the work I do.
If I'm not fully immersed in the world of programming, you will find me hiking in the countryside or at the beach with my dog Lucy.
